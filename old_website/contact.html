Poolesville High School

Poolesville High School, the only whole school magnet in Montgomery County, uniting close-knit, rural community values and diverse global perspectives, challenges its students to become confident, versatile, problem-solving citizens through rigorous cross-curricular pathways and meaningful, motivating learning experiences.

FIRST

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) holds a series of competitions for kids of all ages. These competitions encourage students to combine science, technology, and their imagination to design and build robots to solve problems. It aims to foster teamwork and leadership.
About this website

About this Website

Development

This website was developed by the Falcons web team during the summer of 2012 using the Twitter Bootstrap framwork. It is being hosted on a Bluehost server. The blog is a Wordpress blog, and the forum is hosted by freeforums.org.

Updates

Website and blog updates are the responsibility of the Falcons public relations team. There are multiple writers working with the web team to keep the website as up to date as possible.

Future Plans

In the near future, the Falcons hope to convert their whole site to either a Drupal, Joomla, or Wordpress theme so that content management is made simpler and more efficient.
