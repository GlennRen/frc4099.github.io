Become a Sponsor

We need your help!

Here is our budget breakdown:

  • $5,000 - Chesapeake Regional Registration Fee
  • $4,000 - DC Regional Registration Fee
  • $4,000 - Virginia Regional Registration Fee
  • $5,000 - Championship Registration Fee
  • $4,000 - Robot Costs
  • $3,000 - Tools and Pit Costs
  • $5,000 - Transportation and Food Costs
  • Approximated Total: $30,000

Other than financial aid, we would also appreciate any supplies or services you can provide. Our robot needs a wide variety of parts that does not come with the limited building kit provided by the competition. Services we need include the making of accessories, team shirts, hats, buttons, banners, etc. If your company can provide milling, welding, or other machine shop time, it would be greatly appreciated.


So what's in it for you?

  1. Diamond (Services/Supplies): All of the Platinum benefits.
  2. Platinum ($500+): All of the Titanium benefits, and your name becomes part of our official team name as it’s read at competitions. (For example, “NASA / JCPenney / SAIC / Lockheed Martin / 8020 & Penn-Air / Morningstar Welding / Cabcraft / Bob's Bikes / Poolesville Hardware & Poolesville High School”)
  3. Titanium ($250): All of the Gold benefits, and your name or logo will be displayed on our 2013 robot, which is currently being built!
  4. Gold ($100): All of the Silver plus benefits, and your name or logo will be displayed on the team’s 2013 T-shirts. (We’ll send you one, too.)
  5. Silver ($50): All of the Bronze benefits, plus your name and/or logo is displayed on our pit display at competitions and demonstrations through the year.
  6. Bronze ($25): Your name and/or logo will be displayed on falconsfirst.com’s sponsor page.

*We will also send all sponsors a free T-shirt!


If you would like to donate, please fill out this form and send it to our email or our address (see Contact page). Your donation is 100% tax deductible.

Monetary Sponsors


As a second-year team, we were awarded this sustaining grant of $5,000 which will be spent on the registration fee for our first regional competition. Furthermore, we are very fortunate to have NASA’s continuing support this year.

Poolesville High School

Poolesville High School, the only whole school magnet in Montgomery County, uniting close-knit, rural community values and diverse global perspectives, challenges its students to become confident, versatile, problem-solving citizens through rigorous cross-curricular pathways and meaningful, motivating learning experiences.

FIRST

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) holds a series of competitions for kids of all ages. These competitions encourage students to combine science, technology, and their imagination to design and build robots to solve problems. It aims to foster teamwork and leadership.
About this website

About this Website

Development

This website was developed by the Falcons web team during the summer of 2012 using the Twitter Bootstrap framwork. It is being hosted on a Bluehost server. The blog is a Wordpress blog, and the forum is hosted by freeforums.org.

Updates

Website and blog updates are the responsibility of the Falcons public relations team. There are multiple writers working with the web team to keep the website as up to date as possible.

Future Plans

In the near future, the Falcons hope to convert their whole site to either a Drupal, Joomla, or Wordpress theme so that content management is made simpler and more efficient.
